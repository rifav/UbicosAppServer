Apply your Model

I love this color so much, I might use it for other rooms in my house, but I’m not sure yet exactly how many cups I’ll need. In your group, use the Unifix Tool blocks to create as many models as you can. What are examples of how I could apply this color to any room in my house?

Brainstorm your examples on a whiteboard.
Then, add a table to your whiteboard to show possible quantities that would create the same color of purple paint.

  • How many cups of red and blue paint would I need if it took 100 cups to paint the whole house? 275 cups? Just one cup to paint a doll house?
  • If I want to scale up and use the Perfect Purple Paint on my whole house, I’m not sure I want to do a physical model with blocks for numbers that big. Write a rule we could use to help us find how much paint would be needed without having to make a physical model.
