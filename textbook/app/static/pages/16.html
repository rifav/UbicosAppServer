Paint Splash Gallery Discussion

Take a screenshot of your group work on Paint Splash and upload the picture to the Paint Splash Gallery.

Take a look at other groups work to observe their paint splashes, tables, graphs, and rules. Leave a comment on each picture. You can ask a question, give a compliment, or explain what you are thinking. Be specific. Do you have any questions about what you see? Is there any help you can give to the group? Anything you liked or thought was creative?

After you have commented individually, move to the Paint Splash digital group discussion. Discuss the similarities and differences between the graphs. What features do they share? Why do you think they share these features?

Use the badge option tool inside the gallery to earn badges!
