To join the Khan Academy discussion, follow these instructions:
  • Check your help-giving options here. Select a badge you want to try.
  • After selecting a badge, click the 'Copy to Clipboard' button to copy the sentence starter for you.
  • Now, head to the Ratio Assignment discussion, and help others. You can directly paste your sentence starters in the textbox when you are ready to post.
  • If you want try other badges, visit Modelbook and follow the same process!
